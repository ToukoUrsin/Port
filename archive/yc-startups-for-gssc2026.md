## Top 15 Most Competition-Relevant Ideas (standalone, not our 12)

These are YC ideas that could literally be pitched at GSSC 2026 and score well:

1. **Eggnog (W24)** — Separate platform for AI content. Novel framing: don't fight AI content, give it its own home.
2. **Forum (W26)** — Regulated attention exchange. Makes the invisible attention economy visible and fair.
3. **Didit (W26)** — Universal human verification. The identity layer the internet needs.
4. **Uplift AI (S25)** — Voice AI for underrepresented languages. Infrastructure against cultural erasure.
5. **Pixley AI (F25)** — Families co-create cartoons. Democratizes animation, fights audience passivity.
6. **Lore (S24)** — Real conversations become shareable stories. Authentic human content.
7. **Doomersion (W26)** — Weaponize doomscrolling for language learning. Judo move on attention economy.
8. **Lokal (S19)** — Local news for underserved communities. Proven model (India), applicable globally.
9. **Durioo (W22)** — Cultural representation in children's streaming. Underserved massive market.
10. **Tarjimly (W18)** — Translation for refugees. Humanitarian angle + proven nonprofit model.
11. **Senso (W24)** — Control what AI says about you. Brand-new category of trust problem.
12. **truthsystems (S25)** — Real-time AI governance agents. Automated trust enforcement.
13. **Parrot (F25)** — TikTok format for language learning. Proven engagement + cultural value.
14. **Y/n (S23)** — Interactive stories where audience decides. Anti-passivity platform.
15. **Flick (F25)** — AI filmmaking accessible to everyone. "Figma + Cursor for film."

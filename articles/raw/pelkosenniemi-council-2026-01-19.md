## Key Agenda Items

### Section 5: School Transport Dispute
Taksi Petteri Kokko Oy was excluded from school transport bidding (2026-2028). Company appealed to Market Court. Key facts:
- Company's representative committed "serious professional misconduct" in previous school transport contract
- Company left VAT register (Oct 31, 2025) and advance tax register (Nov 30, 2025)
- Company had no registered board of directors
- Promised to register board by Dec 1, 2025 — didn't
- Emergency contracts made with Taksi Jari Aho and Taksipalvelut Mika Kortelainen to keep kids getting to school
- Company demanding 150,000 EUR penalty from municipality
- Municipality unanimously rejected the appeal

### Section 12: 2026 Budget Implementation
Council approved budget implementation guidelines for 2026. Departments must finalize spending plans by end of February 2026.

### Section 13: Tourist Tax Consultation
Finnish government exploring regional tourist tax for Northern/Eastern Finland (Pyhä-Luosto ski resort area). Pelkosenniemi provided official statement. Part of PM Orpo's Eastern/Northern Finland development program. Would require new tax legislation.

### Section 14: Official Announcement Newspaper 2026
Koti-Lappi newspaper chosen as official municipal announcement paper. Note: Koti-Lappi became a paid subscription paper in 2025 (was previously free distribution). Fewer residents now see official announcements.

### Section 15: East Lapland Economic Vitality Program 2025-2029
Municipality provided statement on regional economic development plan.

### Section 16: Emergency Preparedness Working Group
New emergency preparedness and readiness planning working group established.

### Section 17: Zoning Change — Arokero Property
Response to Northern Finland Administrative Court regarding zoning change on property Arokero 583-403-2-46.

### Section 20: Council Initiative — Financial Situation Report
Councilor Kallio requested a report on the municipality's financial situation.

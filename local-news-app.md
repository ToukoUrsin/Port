# Local News App — Idea Draft

## Concept

A hyperlocal newspaper app where communities create and consume local news. Different areas have their own threads/sections. Locals write articles with AI assistance, making content creation fast and accessible for anyone.

## How It Works

1. **Area-based threads** — each neighborhood/district/city has its own section
2. **AI-assisted writing** — locals write articles with AI help, lowering the barrier so anyone can publish quality content quickly
3. **Community-driven** — the people who live there are the reporters

## Challenge Fit (PORT_ / GSSC 2026: Culture & Media)

Addresses key challenge problems:
- **Cultural erasure (#4)** — local stories and languages get pushed out by dominant platforms
- **Audience passivity (#10)** — people consume but don't shape narratives in their communities
- **Digital divide in creation tools (#8)** — AI removes the skill barrier for writing
- **Creator economy (#3)** — writers get paid for local contributions

## Strengths

- Real problem: local journalism is dying globally
- AI writing makes content creation nearly free and fast
- Low barrier attracts more writers than traditional journalism
- Fills a gap that mainstream media has abandoned
- Strong social impact angle (community empowerment, representation)

## Open Questions / Risks

- **Monetization** — local news is notoriously hard to monetize (local ads pay little, subscriptions convert poorly)
- **Cold start problem** — need writers AND readers in the same area simultaneously
- **Differentiation** — how does this beat Nextdoor, Reddit local subs, Facebook Groups?
- **Writer motivation** — easy to write ≠ motivation to write; what drives people to contribute?
- **Content quality** — AI-assisted doesn't mean good; how do you maintain editorial standards?
- **Demo challenge** — harder to show impact in a 3-minute hackathon demo vs. a tool like Preflight

## Possible Angle: Combine with Preflight

Use Preflight's quality scoring as the editorial layer — every AI-assisted article gets a quality score before publishing. This adds technical depth and a unique differentiator over existing local platforms.
